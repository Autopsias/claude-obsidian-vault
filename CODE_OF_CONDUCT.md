# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**
- Using inclusive and welcoming language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**
- Deliberate disrespect or personal attacks
- Trolling or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
