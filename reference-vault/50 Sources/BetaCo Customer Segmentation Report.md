## Summary

Customer segmentation analysis produced by [[Claire Dubois]] to support the Customer Comms strategy. Segments BetaCo's 847 customer accounts by revenue tier, health score, relationship sensitivity, and change-of-control status. Provides the analytical foundation for the tiered announcement approach.

## Key findings

- **Revenue distribution:** Top 50 accounts represent 68% of BetaCo ARR (£12.2M of £18M). The long tail (597 accounts) represents 11% of ARR.
- **Health scores:** 12 accounts in the "Amber" health band (elevated churn risk signals) — mix of mid-tier accounts experiencing onboarding friction or recent support tickets. These accounts are disproportionately sensitive to announcement uncertainty.
- **Change-of-control accounts:** 3 accounts with change-of-control clauses also happen to be in the top 10 by ARR. Combined ARR: £1.51M. Risk: if any of these do not consent to novation, the revenue loss is material.
  - Nordex Retail: £800K ARR, 3-year contract, 18 months remaining. Primary concern: service level continuity.
  - Brightway Financial: £420K ARR, regulated entity, 2-year contract, 14 months remaining. Primary concern: data handling under new ownership.
  - ClearPath Logistics: £290K ARR, 1-year contract, 8 months remaining. Primary concern: pricing stability.
- **Announcement sensitivity:** 73 accounts (8.6%) have had direct communication with [[Emma Walsh]] as CEO in the past 12 months. These accounts will expect personal outreach regardless of revenue tier.
- **Tier 1 (personal outreach):** 50 accounts — top 50 by ARR + 12 Amber-band accounts not already in the top 50.
- **Tier 2 (personalised email):** 200 accounts — mid-tier, green health scores.
- **Tier 3 (mass email):** 597 accounts — long tail.

## Implications for Project Atlas

The Amber-band and Emma-relationship accounts need to be elevated to Tier 1 treatment. The 3 change-of-control accounts need pre-announcement legal outreach. The 12 Amber-band accounts need proactive CS outreach before the general announcement, regardless of when the announcement lands.
