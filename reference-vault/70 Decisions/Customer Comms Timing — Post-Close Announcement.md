## Decision

General customer announcement to be made post-close, targeting 2026-03-15 (Day 59). Pre-close announcement window has passed (deal closed 2026-01-15). Change-of-control accounts (Nordex Retail, Brightway Financial, ClearPath Logistics) to receive legal outreach before the general announcement. Tiered customer outreach approach: Tier 1 personal calls (top 50 accounts + 12 Amber-band accounts), Tier 2 personalised email (next 200), Tier 3 mass email (remaining 597).

## Rationale

Pre-close announcement was not feasible given the deal confidentiality requirements during SPA negotiation. Post-close, the priority is to get ahead of the news cycle before press coverage intensifies. 2026-03-15 (Day 59) gives enough time to complete the change-of-control legal outreach (from 2026-03-08) and prepare personalised messaging for Tier 1 accounts, without waiting so long that customers feel deliberately kept in the dark.

## Alternatives considered

1. **Immediate post-close announcement (Day 1–2):** Announce on 2026-01-16. Rejected — insufficient time to prepare tiered outreach, personalise messaging for top accounts, or coordinate with legal on change-of-control notices. A mass email on Day 2 would feel impersonal and generate inbound volume that CS could not handle.
2. **Day 90 announcement (aligned with SSO cutover):** Announce when the SSO migration is complete. Rejected — waiting until Day 90 risks press discovery in the interim; BetaCo customers discovering the acquisition from a press article rather than from their account manager would be a worse outcome than a Day 59 announcement with a work-in-progress message.
3. **Pre-announcement to all change-of-control accounts simultaneously with general announcement:** Rejected on legal advice ([[Sophie Andreou]], [[Anna Fischer]]) — change-of-control clauses require notice, not a simultaneous announcement; legal outreach must precede the general announcement.

## Implications

- Announcement draft due to [[Morgan Chen]] from [[Emma Walsh]] by 2026-03-08.
- Change-of-control legal outreach begins week of 2026-03-08 (7 days before general announcement).
- [[Claire Dubois]] to complete proactive CS outreach to 12 Amber-band accounts before 2026-03-15.
- Announcement messaging must align with [[Linda Park]]'s Q2 earnings call narrative (April 2026).

## Counter-arguments

[[Claire Dubois]] argued that the pre-close window should have been used to signal customers informally before deal close. This was not possible without breaching deal confidentiality. Her underlying concern — that customers will feel blindsided — is valid and addressed through the tiered outreach approach and the Amber-band elevation to Tier 1.
