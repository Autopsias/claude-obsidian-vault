## Overview

Data architecture and cloud consultancy. Specialises in Azure-based data platform design and UK GDPR compliance architecture. Led by [[James Whitfield]] (partner). ~30 consultants; headquartered in London. Engaged by Acme Corp to validate and design the Data Residency architecture for Project Atlas.

## Relationship to Project Atlas

Data architecture and compliance validation provider. Sigma Partners produced the technical due diligence on BetaCo's data platform and designed the UK-Stay architecture (Azure UK South for BetaCo/UK data, Azure East US for Acme/US data). Their engagement is fixed-fee (£180K SOW) and runs through the Data Residency architecture sign-off, expected by Day 75 (2026-03-31).
