## Overview

UK SaaS startup, ~150 employees, headquartered in London (Shoreditch). Founded 2018 by [[Emma Walsh]] and [[Raj Patel]]. Product: customer lifecycle analytics platform — tracks customer health scores, churn signals, and engagement metrics for B2B SaaS companies. Pre-acquisition ARR: £18M; gross margin 68%. Acquired by Acme Corp on 2026-01-15. Now operating as a business unit within Acme; formal legal entity transition to Acme Corp UK Ltd is in progress via Contract Novation.

## Relationship to Project Atlas

Acquiree. BetaCo's 150 employees, 847 customer contracts, 23 vendor contracts, and full technology stack are the subject of the integration. Key tensions: BetaCo team's cultural identity (startup vs corporate), [[Tom Bradley]]'s retention risk, and the 3 change-of-control clauses in customer contracts. BetaCo's data platform (designed by [[Raj Patel]]) is architecturally distinct from Acme's — the long-term product integration path is not yet decided.
