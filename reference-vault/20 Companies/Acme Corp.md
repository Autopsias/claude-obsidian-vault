## Overview

US enterprise SaaS company, ~3,000 employees, headquartered in San Francisco. Listed on NASDAQ (ticker: ACME). Core product: B2B CRM and sales analytics platform. FY2025 revenue: $380M ARR; gross margin 74%. Acquired BetaCo on 2026-01-15 for $94M enterprise value, funded from existing cash reserves and a $60M credit facility draw.

## Relationship to Project Atlas

Acquirer. The integration is being run from Acme's side with [[Morgan Chen]] (CIO) as integration lead. Key internal stakeholders: [[Linda Park]] (CEO, sponsor), [[David Kim]] (CFO, budget holder), [[Lisa Chen]] (VP Engineering, BetaCo engineering home TBD). The 18-month synergy target ($12M in COGS savings) is David Kim's primary success metric.
