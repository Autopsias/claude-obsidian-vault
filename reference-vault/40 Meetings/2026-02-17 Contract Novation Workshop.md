## Key discussion points

- [[Sophie Andreou]] presented the Freshfields novation template. Standard form covers ~829 of 847 contracts (Tiers 3–4). 18 contracts require individual review (Tiers 1–2). 3 require customer consent (change-of-control clauses).
- [[Anna Fischer]] flagged 5 vendor contracts that BetaCo negotiated with startup-preferential terms that Acme will likely lose on renegotiation: "Expect pushback from these vendors once they see Acme's name on the agreement."
- [[Emma Walsh]] raised the question of timing: "Can we complete novation before the general customer announcement?" Sophie: "No — the novation template requires the announcement to already be in the market. Customers need to know who Acme Corp UK Ltd is before they sign a novation letter from them."
- Change-of-control accounts discussed: Nordex Retail (concern: service level continuity), Brightway Financial (concern: data handling post-acquisition), ClearPath Logistics (concern: pricing stability). [[Anna Fischer]] has an existing relationship with all three legal teams.
- [[Ben Miller]] noted that 3 of the 5 vendor renegotiations involve BetaCo's primary cloud tooling — may affect BetaCo engineering team workflow during transition.

- Change-of-control outreach to Nordex, Brightway, ClearPath: [[Anna Fischer]] leads, [[Sophie Andreou]] advises. Outreach before general announcement.
- Vendor renegotiation: [[Ben Miller]] to project-manage; target completion Day 90.
